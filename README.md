# Abstract

The Ocean Tuning Unit (OTU) is an autonomous, nature-complementary artificial organ designed to reboot Earth's stagnant vertical ocean circulation.

By integrating:

* an Archimedean Spiral Air Injector,
* a Tensegrity-based Deep Sea Pipe,
* buoyancy-driven convection,
* and distributed renewable energy harvesting,

the OTU induces large-scale upwelling to restore marine ecosystems and provide direct planetary cooling.

This framework moves beyond:

> “human-centric carbon reduction”

toward:

> “nature-complementary Earth tuning.”

The OTU is designed not merely as a machine, but as a distributed environmental regulation infrastructure capable of restoring planetary-scale circulation systems.

# 1. Structural Architecture: Octahedron Geometry

The OTU utilizes an:

# Octahedron (Dual-Pyramid) Structure

as its primary buoyancy and energy-harvesting platform.

This geometry is selected for:

* Structural stability
* Load distribution efficiency
* Ocean current resistance
* Modular scalability
* Multi-directional energy harvesting

---

## Top Pyramid — Energy Acquisition Layer

The upper pyramid is covered with:

* Perovskite Solar Cells
* Protective transparent acrylic layers

This structure mimics:

> the waxy cuticle layer of plant leaves,

allowing:

* weather resistance,
* saltwater protection,
* and high-efficiency solar collection.

---

## Quadrangular Girdle — Wind Energy Layer

The central girdle region integrates:

# Vertical Axis Wind Turbines (VAWT)

positioned at four corners.

Advantages include:

* Multi-directional wind capture
* Low-speed wind operation
* Ocean storm survivability
* Reduced mechanical stress
* Distributed torque balance

---

## Submerged Pyramid — Tidal Energy Layer

The lower submerged pyramid integrates:

# Vertical Axis Tidal Turbines

to harvest kinetic energy from:

* ocean currents,
* vertical flow,
* and tidal movement.

This creates:

# Multi-Layer Renewable Energy Harvesting

within a single structural body.

# 2. Tuning Core: Spiral-Driven Forced Air Injection

Conventional deep-sea aeration systems require extremely high-pressure compressors to overcome approximately:

# 25 atmospheres of pressure at 250m depth.

The OTU instead proposes:

# Spiral Displacement Injection

using a mechanical Archimedean transport system.

---

## Mechanism

An internal:

# Archimedean Spiral Screw

rotates at:

* low speed,
* high torque,

physically transporting:

* air-water packets
* as discrete volumetric units.

---

## Efficiency Logic

Unlike conventional pneumatic compression:

* pressure resistance is reduced,
* energy loss is minimized,
* and mechanical displacement replaces brute-force air compression.

This allows:

# Sustainable Deep-Sea Aeration

powered primarily by:

* solar energy,
* wind energy,
* and tidal energy.

---

# 3. Propulsion Logic: Buoyancy-Driven Convection

## Deep-Sea Breath Model

The OTU generates massive vertical flow by utilizing:

# Buoyancy amplification through gas expansion.

---

## Nano-Bubble Injection

Ultra-fine nano-bubbles are released at approximately:

# 250m ocean depth.

---

## Autonomous Vertical Acceleration

As bubbles rise:

* external pressure decreases,
* gas volume expands according to Boyle’s Law,
* and buoyancy force increases continuously.

A theoretical:

# ~25x volume expansion

creates a self-accelerating air-lift effect capable of transporting:

* nutrient-rich deep water,
* dissolved minerals,
* and cooled lower-layer water

toward the ocean surface.

---

## Biological Tuning

The OTU may internally cultivate selected non-harmful phytoplankton species such as:

* Chlorella
* Spirulina

These organisms may be released into the upwelling current to enhance:

* biological carbon fixation,
* oxygen production,
* and marine ecosystem recovery.

The ocean itself becomes:

# A Planetary Carbon-Sequestration Organ

rather than a passive heat reservoir.

capable of resisting:
